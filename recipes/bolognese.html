Spaghetti Bolognese

one of the italian classics

A simple italian dish that almost everyone knows! Easy to cook, and tasty as well!

Are your tastebuds ready?

Ingredients (one serving)

  • 1/4 onion
  • 1/4 clove garlic
  • 125g ground beef
  • 50 ml vegetable broth
  • 17.5g tomato paste
  • 1/4 Tbsp oregano
  • 100g chunky tomatoes
  • 1/2 Tbsp ketchup
  • 125g Spaghetti

Steps

  1. First cut onion, garlic and Carrot into fine cubes
  2. Add the ground beef to the pan, heat slowly and sauté in its own fat. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Then add the cut onions, garlic and carrots and sautè briefly. Deglaze with the broth
  4. Add the tomato paste, oregano and chunky tomatoes and mix well. Let it cook for 40 minutes.
  5. Cook the spaghetti in saltwater until done. Serve with the sauce. And you're done!
